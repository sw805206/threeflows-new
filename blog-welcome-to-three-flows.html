Welcome to Three Flows Solutions

Every business is the management of three flows. Our job is to help you master all three — by teaching you what we know, and working beside you when knowing isn't enough.

March 5, 2025

Starting a business has never been easier. Running one has never been more complex. The tools to launch are everywhere; the knowledge to sustain and scale is harder to find — and hardest to apply when you're in the middle of it.

Three Flows Solutions exists to close that gap, with two commitments. Education: helping small businesses become financially savvy and process-savvy — ready to scale, not just to start. Execution: providing professional support through the early stages, when a business can least afford mistakes and can least afford the people who prevent them.

We work with small retailers and brand owners — anyone who buys and sells goods, whether online (marketplaces and webstores), offline (physical retail), or inline (selling through social media feeds and livestreams). Yes, we're coining "inline." Social commerce is no longer a side channel, and it deserves its own word.

The goal, plainly: you focus on your growth, we'll do the rest — your champion, your cheerleader, your attaché, whatever the moment calls for.

Two Sides of the Same Coin

We believe education and entrepreneurship complement each other the way two sides of a coin do — you can't spend one without the other. Book-smart tells you what should work; street-smart tells you what actually does. A business needs both, in the same person, at the same time.

Our version of that principle: plan what you do, and let what you do improve your plan. Every projection gets tested by execution; every result feeds back into a better plan. That's the positive closed loop a healthy business runs on — and it's how we work, whether we're teaching or executing.

The Name Means Something

Most small businesses don't fail because the idea was wrong. They fail because one flow broke — a product that couldn't be sourced reliably, a growing business that ran out of cash at exactly the wrong moment, a team executing hard with no signal on whether it's working.

Every business, regardless of size or model, is fundamentally the management of three flows. Keep all three moving and aligned, and you can scale. Let one stall, and the others eventually follow. Each flow is also where we do our work:

Material flow — the physical movement of goods: sourcing, production, logistics, delivery. Our offering: sourcing support and logistics oversight.

Information flow — the intelligence behind your decisions: market research, customer signals, costs you can actually trust. Our offering: tools for making the right decisions — free and customized — plus connections to vetted resources.

Cash flow — the financial lifeblood: how money moves, where working capital hides, whether you can sustain the run. Our offering: financial planning, reconciliation, and back-office support.
