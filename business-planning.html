Frequently asked questions

How long does the business planning engagement take?
It depends on the package. Starter reports take 1–2 business days; a standard engagement runs 1–2 weeks; a comprehensive one, 2–3 weeks. We agree the timeline together at the discovery call before the clock starts.
How can I reduce the cost and turnaround time?
Come prepared. The more you’ve done up front — competitor research, product specs, a rough cost target, your target customer — the less time we spend on discovery and the faster we reach the analysis that matters.
What if my idea doesn’t pass validation?
That’s a success — you’ve saved yourself a much more expensive mistake. We walk you through exactly what the data shows and what would need to change to make the idea viable.
What’s the difference between this and a business plan?
A business plan is a document — this is a process backed by real expertise and live data, with an expert walking you through the logic behind every number.
